hatshepsut temple

the funerary temple of hatshepsut, which was built during the reign of the pharaoh hatshepsut of the eighteenth dynasty of egypt located opposite the city of luxor, it is considered to be masterpiece of ancient architecture.

abu simbel temple

a historic site comprising two massive rock-cut temples in vallage of abu simbel, aswan governorate, near the border with sudan, the twin temples were originally carved out of the mountainside in the 13th century BC.
